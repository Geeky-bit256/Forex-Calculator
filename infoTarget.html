Calculating Target

Deposit Percentage Target
R1,000 1,000% R9,000
R1,000 10,000% R99,000
R1,000 100,000% R999,000

When calculating the target, it's essential to note that your initial deposit is not included in the calculation. This might seem counterintuitive at first, but it's crucial to understand the distinction.

For example, let's say you deposit R1,000 and you're aiming for a 1,000% return on your investment. In this scenario, your target amount would be R9,000. This means that if you reach your target, your total balance would be R10,000 (your initial deposit of R1,000 + the R9,000 profit).

To illustrate this further, consider the following:

  • Your deposit is the initial amount you invest, in this case, R1,000.
  • The percentage return (1,000% in our example) represents the profit you're aiming to make on your investment.
  • The target amount (R9,000) is the profit you need to make to reach your desired return, excluding your initial deposit.

By understanding this concept, you'll be able to accurately calculate your target amounts and make informed decisions about your investments.
