Calculating Target

Deposit Percentage Target
R1,000 1,000% R9,000
R1,000 10,000% R99,000
R1,000 100,000% R999,000

When calculating the target you dont include your deposit.For example if you deposit R1,000 and you target 1,000% you are targeting R9,000 .If you include your capital it will be R10,000
