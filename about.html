About

The Investment Calculator is a simple yet powerful tool designed to help you make informed decisions about your investments. With this intuitive calculator, you can easily calculate two essential metrics:

  • Target Calculation: Determine the target amount you need to reach based on your initial deposit and desired percentage return. Simply input your deposit amount and the percentage you want to target, and our calculator will do the rest.
  • Percentage Return Calculation: Calculate the percentage return on your investment based on your initial deposit and the profit you've made. Enter your starting deposit and the profit you've earned, and our calculator will quickly calculate the percentage return on your investment.
